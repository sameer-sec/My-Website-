08 // FAQ

How long does the full production take?

Script-only projects are delivered within 7 business days. Full Script to Screen production takes 3–4 weeks from the Discovery session. Retained partners receive priority turnaround of 10 business days per VSL. Timelines assume prompt approval at each stage.

What format will my VSL be in?

Full production delivers MP4, WebM, and source files — ready for landing pages, funnels, YouTube, Meta, TikTok, or any platform you're running. We also provide a closed captions file. Format preference can be discussed at the Discovery stage.

Do I need to be on camera?

No. Lockscroll produces animated explainer VSLs, motion-graphic VSLs, and talking-head formats. The format recommendation comes out of Discovery — it's whichever best serves your offer and audience. Most clients go with animated or motion-graphic production.

What if I already have a script?

Bring it to a Discovery call. We'll review it honestly. If it's conversion-ready, we'll work with it. If it has structural gaps — unclear hook, missing objection handling, weak close — we'll tell you what needs fixing and quote accordingly.

What makes Lockscroll different from a general video agency?

Most video agencies produce content. Lockscroll produces conversion assets. The difference is in the copy architecture — every script is built on AIDA, PAS, and our proprietary narrative arc specifically designed to move a viewer from problem-aware to ready-to-buy. We obsess over conversion, not just production quality.

What do I need to provide to get started?

Book the Free VSL Strategy Call. Come with your offer details, your target customer, and a sense of where your current funnel is breaking down. We'll do the rest — structured brief, audience research, competitive analysis, and the full production from there.
